illustration about & contact

Originating from southeastern Wisconsin, Walt Reid is an illustrator who works with ink and paint to make the fantastic seem possible. He spent his youth by firelight, sheltered from midwinter frosts playing board games with his cousins. There he was given an education in the bizarre and armed with an obsession for imaginary worlds: he’d take these to the Milwaukee Institute of Art and Design, graduating in 2025 with a BFA in Illustration. His work has been featured in WYRD Magazine, a Milwaukee-based anthology of fantasy art. When he isn’t drawing, he’s most often found on a trail or in a book.

If you’d like to locate him, send professional inquiries to walt.b.reid@gmail.com.
