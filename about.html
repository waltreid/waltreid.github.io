Walt Reid is an illustrator from southeastern Wisconsin who works with pen and paintbrush to make windows between the fantastic and the real.

Sometime in high school, trying to escape a summer storm, his cousins led him straight into a hole full of goblins. Over tea, they educated him on imaginary worlds, and gave him a pencil to guard against mundanity. Thusly armed, he traveled to the Milwaukee Institute of Art and Design, graduating in 2025 with a BFA in Illustration. When he isn’t drawing, he’s most often lost in a book or on a trail.

Professional inquiries sent to walt.b.reid@gmail.com will reach him, nonetheless.
